Time is Limited

Aug 04th in TIME SERIES by bro James AbahPub Sec

TIME IS LIMITED
If time is TRULY limited, then what is Life?
Life is a vapor that appear for a little time and then vanishes away? If someone had told me years ago that life is too short and will vanishes, I wouldn't have believed and If I get to tell you now you might not believe either.

For Billy Graham once said "I cannot tell young people how brief life is "How quickly it passes" It even seems like yesterday we were given this mantle of servitude right in All soul's chapel in that very evening, Calculating this time in The human calendars is certainly 11 months and a month to a year.

Breaking this down it means; We have so far spent 11 months= 11 months * 30 = 330 days. How have you spent each of these days? ... Truly by providence we have been given the same amount of time in a day i.e 1440 minutes in a day, 168 hours in a week, God has given us a limited time even with the advancement in Health, Science and Technology we have not averagely exceed this figure. Average American male lives 70 years, female lives 73 years and 6 months Average Nigerian lives 54.69 years (2019) More live beyond this figure, and others below the figure above but the average age is the mean of overall. What a reality it becomes when you think and see you have a short life to spend before it vanishes! I'd write down my priorities in life and I get committed to certain priorities in Life, Now is the accepted time, the things we ought to do, the class we need to take, the books we need to read *DO IT NOW* The family you need to spend more time with, "DO IT NOW" Money you ought to give, do it now. Time to invest in study *DO IT NOW* People you need to evangelize to *DO IT NOW* EACH TIME THE CLOCK TICKS ITS SAYING *NOW!* *NOW!* Today do it now for there may not be tomorrow for you or for me to kick start that Vision committed into our hands to do *NOW* Because there is a warning attached to timing "It is running and will not wait for you* Time is too short for indecision and procrastination. *"BE DECISIVE"* TO A FOOL, Time is long but every morning we have 86400 seconds to spend and invest wisely in a day, and every second counts. Each day the bank called *TIME* opens its NEW Account for you and me and IT allows no OVERDRAFT NOR BALANCE* 86400 IS 86400 deposit in the account for everyy one. If you fail to use today’s DEPOSIT the loss is yours. "USE THE DEPOSIT NOW" The bible says redeem the time because today is evil, If there is ever time for the gospel has to be taught it is NOW. It is the quality of life and not the Length, Our Christ Jesus Spent only 33 years and ended on the cross; to the World he was a failure at that point but at the end of HIs life, He said I finished the work that my Father gavest me. It doesn't mean if you living for let say 2 or 3 or 10 years but will your work be finished? A call for you and I to use our time judiciously! INVEST THAT TIME NOW
